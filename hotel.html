Hotels in Nepal

Hotel Sabrina, also known as Regenta Place Sabrina, is a stylish 4-star hotel located in Budhanilkantha, Kathmandu, nestled beneath the scenic Shivapuri Hills. Just 8 kilometers from Tribhuvan International Airport, it offers convenient access to the city center and nearby cultural landmarks like Pashupatinath Temple and Budhanilkantha Temple. The hotel features a variety of accommodations, including Deluxe Rooms, Premium Rooms, Business Suites, and a luxurious Presidential Suite. Guests can enjoy amenities such as a rooftop swimming pool, spa and wellness center, fitness suite, and halal dining options.
